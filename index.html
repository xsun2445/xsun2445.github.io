Xinghua Sun (孙兴华)

ECE Ph.D. student at University of Washington

Hi! I am currently a 2nd year Ph.D. student at University of Washington in the department of Electrical and Computer Engineering. I am a member of the Networking and Emerging Wireless Technologies Lab (NEWT), studying under the supervision of Prof. Akshay Gadre.

I am passionate about solving exciting and impactful real-world challenges. Recently My research interest lies in Millimeter-Wave sensing and imaging system.

Prior to PhD, I received my Master’s degree in Electrical Engineering from Columbia University (2022) worked with Prof. Fred Jiang at Intelligent and Connected Systems Lab (ICSL) working on embedded AI. I received my Bachelor’s degree in Automation Science and Engineering from South China University of Technology (2021).
